## purpose
Classify inbound demand quickly so high-fit leads get fast follow-up and low-fit or blocked leads do not consume seller time.

## when to use
Use immediately after a form fill, demo request, reply-to-marketing message, or routed inbound email.

## operator steps
1. Run do-not-send and compliance checks first.
2. Score the lead against ICP and buying-signal rules.
3. Assign the owner, tier, and SLA based on fit and route logic.
4. Notify the owner or queue when the lead is urgent.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Compliance gating happens before engagement, always.
- Tiering should reflect account quality and urgency, not just job title prestige.
- Route ownership must consider territory, segment, and language rules.
- If data is incomplete, flag uncertainty instead of over-prioritizing.

## expected outputs
- A routed inbound record with tier, owner, SLA, and any compliance block reason.
