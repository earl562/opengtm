## purpose
Prepare CS and sales for renewal motion by summarizing health, value delivery, exec coverage, and renewal blockers at key checkpoints.

## when to use
Use at T-90, T-60, and T-30 before renewal date, or before an internal renewal review.

## operator steps
1. Pull health score, usage trend, and open support escalations.
2. Review contract timing, known commercial requests, and exec touch history.
3. Rank the top renewal risks and value proof points.
4. Produce a checkpoint brief with clear follow-up recommendations.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Contract terms and pricing guidance are sensitive; do not improvise finance decisions.
- Distinguish adoption risk from relationship risk.
- Escalations should include status and owner, not just ticket count.
- Show trend direction because static snapshots hide renewal trajectory.

## expected outputs
- A renewal checkpoint brief with top risks, usage direction, exec coverage, and recommended actions.
