## purpose
Give the AE a compact weekly account update that surfaces deal movement, product usage, and org changes without making them hunt across systems.

## when to use
Use on a weekly cadence for active strategic accounts or on demand before internal account review.

## operator steps
1. Pull open deals, stage movement, and task state from CRM.
2. Query product usage deltas and highlight trend changes that matter commercially.
3. Scan meeting intelligence and public org-change signals.
4. Assemble the brief by separating facts, risks, and suggested next moves.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Keep the brief time-bounded so stale information does not look current.
- Numeric product claims need a warehouse-backed source.
- Do not include privileged legal or procurement notes in broad circulation artifacts.
- Surface competitive noise only when it changes account strategy.

## expected outputs
- A weekly account brief with deal state, usage delta, org-change notes, and recommended follow-up areas.
