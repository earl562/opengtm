## purpose
Summarize what competitors are doing around a target account so the AE can adjust discovery, objection handling, and deal strategy.

## when to use
Use when competitor mentions show up in calls or when the rep asks for a recent competitive snapshot.

## operator steps
1. Search recent transcripts and inbox threads for named competitors.
2. Pull recent web signals such as launches, pricing, or messaging shifts.
3. Group findings into threats, weak signals, and rebuttal preparation topics.
4. Write the brief with citations and recency markers.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Stick to evidence; rumor is not intel.
- Never create outward-facing copy that trashes competitors.
- Timebox the lookback window so the brief reflects current motion.
- Separate account-specific mentions from market-wide news.

## expected outputs
- A competitor brief with cited mentions, likely impact on the deal, and rebuttal prep notes.
