## purpose
Give the CS or support operator immediate account context before they respond to an assigned issue.

## when to use
Use when a support ticket is assigned and the responder needs customer history fast.

## operator steps
1. Pull the account, plan, and product footprint.
2. Review recent tickets, escalations, and known bug matches.
3. Add a current usage snapshot and recent deploy history if relevant.
4. Package the context into a response-ready summary.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Keep PII out of broad channels and recap artifacts.
- Prior incidents should inform tone and urgency, not bias the diagnosis.
- Do not flood the responder with raw logs when a summary will do.
- Known-bug matches need confidence language if the issue is not confirmed.

## expected outputs
- A compact support context brief with plan, recent incident history, usage posture, and likely known-bug links.
