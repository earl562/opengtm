## purpose
Build a source-backed lead brief that helps an SDR decide whether the account is worth a first touch and which angle is most credible.

## when to use
Use for new inbound leads, outbound prospecting, or before reviving a stale lead when the rep needs fresh context.

## operator steps
1. Start from the CRM record and confirm company domain, person, and owner.
2. Pull enrichment and public web signals for funding, hiring, launches, and role changes.
3. Check prior meetings or notes so the brief does not repeat solved questions.
4. Summarize the likely pain, current timing signal, and best outreach angle.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Use only evidence you can cite to a source.
- Separate account facts from contact-level assumptions.
- If the signal is weak, say it is weak instead of forcing a narrative.
- Stop short of drafting outreach; this skill hands off to messaging work.

## expected outputs
- A short research brief with citations, buyer role, signal strength, and recommended outreach angle.
