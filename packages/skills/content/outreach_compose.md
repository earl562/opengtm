## purpose
Draft a first-touch outbound message that sounds like the assigned rep and stays grounded in the approved research brief.

## when to use
Use after lead research is complete and before any email or LinkedIn message is sent.

## operator steps
1. Load the research brief and rep style memory.
2. Pick one angle worth leading with instead of stacking multiple weak reasons.
3. Draft a short subject line, body, and single CTA.
4. Route the draft to a human for send, edit, or cancel.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Never send automatically.
- Keep cold openers concise; do not turn them into mini case studies.
- Do not reference internal notes, non-public customer details, or unverified claims.
- If personalization is thin, prefer a cleaner generic angle over fake specificity.

## expected outputs
- An approval-ready outreach draft with one clear CTA and a trace back to the research source.
