## purpose
Expose late-stage deal risks early enough for the AE or manager to intervene before forecast quality degrades.

## when to use
Use daily on proposal-stage and later deals, or before forecast review.

## operator steps
1. Load the open late-stage pipeline cohort.
2. Check contact silence, MEDDPICC completeness, and stalled-stage duration.
3. Scan transcripts or notes for competitor mentions and buying friction.
4. Produce a ranked risk summary with evidence and next-action ideas.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Evidence matters more than a raw score.
- A silent champion is risky only in context; recent procurement activity can offset it.
- Do not auto-downgrade or upgrade forecast categories without rep confirmation.
- Avoid duplicate risk reasons that describe the same root cause.

## expected outputs
- A per-deal risk list with composite score, evidence, and intervention suggestions.
