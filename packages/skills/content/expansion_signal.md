## purpose
Spot account growth moments that justify expansion outreach while keeping CS and AE coordination intact.

## when to use
Use on healthy customer accounts when usage, team spread, or hiring suggests room to grow.

## operator steps
1. Measure user, seat, and feature adoption growth.
2. Look for spread into new teams or business units.
3. Add external hiring or initiative signals that support a budget story.
4. Recommend a coordinated expansion play and owner.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Do not trigger expansion pressure during a fragile renewal or active escalation without CS alignment.
- Usage spikes need context; one launch week is not durable expansion intent.
- Separate true expansion potential from simple onboarding completion.
- Recommend the play, not the send.

## expected outputs
- An expansion opportunity note with the strongest signals, timing caveats, and suggested owner/action.
