## purpose
Maintain a transparent account health score that Customer Success can explain, defend, and act on.

## when to use
Use on a daily or weekly cadence when the workspace wants durable account-level health tracking.

## operator steps
1. Gather the weighted inputs for usage, support burden, NPS, and executive engagement.
2. Normalize each component to the configured scale.
3. Combine the components into a 0-100 score.
4. Store the score with component explanations and recent change notes.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- A score without component transparency is not actionable.
- Avoid early alarms during onboarding or other warmup windows.
- Do not hide missing data behind false precision.
- Trend direction matters as much as current absolute score.

## expected outputs
- A health score artifact with weighted components, explanation text, and change-over-time context.
