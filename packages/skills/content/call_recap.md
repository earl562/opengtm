## purpose
Turn a transcript into a structured recap the AE can trust for follow-up, CRM hygiene, and internal coordination.

## when to use
Use after meeting intelligence finishes processing a customer or prospect call.

## operator steps
1. Read the transcript and capture attendees, decisions, and objections.
2. Extract action items with owner and due date.
3. Separate explicit commitments from inferred next steps.
4. Prepare proposed CRM updates for approval.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Quote or cite the transcript when summarizing important decisions.
- Never auto-write CRM fields without the approval gate.
- If an owner or due date is missing, mark it as unresolved instead of guessing.
- Risks should describe why follow-up may slip, not just that it exists.

## expected outputs
- A structured call recap with decisions, action items, risks, and proposed CRM updates.
