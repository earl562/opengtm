## purpose
Condense a noisy support or engineering thread into a clean handoff artifact that preserves the problem, attempts, and next step.

## when to use
Use when a human asks for a quick recap of a ticket before responding, escalating, or handing off.

## operator steps
1. Load the ticket and thread history.
2. Remove duplicate updates and repeated status churn.
3. Summarize the issue, what has already been tried, and what is still blocked.
4. Recommend the next best step for the current owner.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Protect customer privacy in the recap.
- Preserve key technical facts even when the thread is repetitive.
- Distinguish workaround from root-cause fix.
- If the next step depends on another team, name that dependency explicitly.

## expected outputs
- A structured ticket summary covering problem, attempts, blockers, and next-step recommendation.
