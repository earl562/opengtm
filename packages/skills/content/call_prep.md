## purpose
Give the AE a one-page pre-call brief that compresses history, open actions, and usage context into a usable meeting prep artifact.

## when to use
Use in the hour before a customer or prospect meeting once attendees are mapped to CRM.

## operator steps
1. Pull meeting metadata, attendee roles, and last-call recap.
2. Review open tasks, deal stage, and unresolved questions.
3. Add relevant product usage or support signals for the current agenda.
4. Assemble a concise prep page with priorities and watchouts.

## gtm constraints and gotchas
- Keep it brief enough to scan in minutes.
- Highlight changes since the last interaction, not the whole account history.
- Do not bury action items under generic meeting context.
- Missing attendee mapping should be called out because it changes prep quality.

## expected outputs
- A one-page call prep brief with attendees, last-touch recap, open actions, and usage context.
